DESTINED DEATH

Equipment & Magic/Skills

Destined Death is a Skill in Elden Ring. Destined Death allows the user to create a myriad of blades, to deal damage. Updated to Patch 1.07.

ELDEN RING DESTINED DEATH GUIDE

This is a Unique Skill
FP Cost: 40
This Skill is not Chargeable
Reduces enemy max HP by 10% for 45 seconds.
Deals 17 stance damage.

HOW TO GET DESTINED DEATH IN ELDEN RING

Unique skill for Maliketh's Black Blade.
